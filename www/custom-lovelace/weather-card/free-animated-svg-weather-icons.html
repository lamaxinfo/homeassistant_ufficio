Free animated SVG weather icons

We’re all about beautiful vector stuff here at amCharts. That’s why we felt compelled to create our own, how-grown and nurtured icons for our weather demos and especially Weather Map tool.

We also want you to use them in your projects as well. Free. No strings attached.

All icons are fully scaleable, based on vector-based standard SVG and animated using CSS.
